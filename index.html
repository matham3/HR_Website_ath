People & Culture

KAUST's mission depends on attracting, developing, and retaining exceptional talent.

People & Culture serves as a strategic partner and center of excellence, aligning talent, leadership, and culture with KAUST's goals for research, innovation, and operational excellence.


Through five pillars, we build strong leadership, engaged teams, and data-driven insights that sustain KAUST's global competitiveness.

Our Five Pillars

Attract & Retain World-Class Talent

Recruit and retain exceptional global and national talent to advance KAUST's research, innovation, and operational excellence.

Develop & Grow Future-Ready Capabilities

Strengthen leadership pipelines and technical expertise to deliver institutional priorities and Vision 2030 impact.

Strengthen Culture & Engagement

Foster a transparent, collaborative environment that cultivates productivity, innovation, and institutional alignment.

Align & Enable High Performance

Drive performance excellence by linking goals, feedback, and rewards to strategic priorities, promoting accountability, development, and growth.

Ensure Competitive Value Proposition

Maintain competitive compensation and benefits that reward performance and reinforce KAUST's position as an employer of choice.
