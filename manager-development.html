Manager Development

Building leadership capability at KAUST

Develop Your Management Skills

When leaders develop, their people management skills are strengthened. They discover resources for day-to-day work, build skills to set expectations and deliver coaching feedback, and become more confident in their management capabilities.

Here you can discover different learning opportunities through our up to date workshops in addition to other resources that can add value to your current skills.

Manager Essentials – Power Hour Sessions

Manager Essentials sessions are monthly, hour-long webinars fostering collaborative learning among peers in management roles at KAUST.

These sessions enhance management skills and equip participants with tools to implement organizational processes throughout the employee lifecycle, including probation management, onboarding, performance evaluation, and professional development.

Open to all with people management responsibilities, these HR-facilitated webinars include discussions, Q&A, and post-session resources. No registration is required; managers can attend at the scheduled time.

Leadership Conversations – Workshop Series

What is the Leadership Conversations Workshop Series?

  • To support the socialization and embedding of the Leadership Principles, various learning opportunities will be offered across the university in the coming months. These opportunities complement the activity that is already taking place at a departmental / divisional level.
  • Leadership Conversations is a series of workshops aimed at exploring the behaviors, mindset and skills needed to lead and manage at KAUST. The workshops will provide opportunities for peer discussion, engagement and learning.

Which topics will be covered?

There will be many topics over the coming months – the first workshop is highlighted below.

Leadership Conversations: Introduction to the Leadership Principles

Workshop objectives

  • Opportunity to reflect on personal views and perspectives on leadership
  • Understand the background to the development of the Leadership Principles, how the statements were created and why they are seen to be of critical importance to the long-term leadership of KAUST
  • Connect with peers to discuss current practice in relation to the Leadership Principles and consider how the principles influence the roles of the KAUST leader
  • Identify tangible steps to integrate the principles on a daily basis; think about the mindset, knowledge and skills required to be a strong KAUST Leader and identify opportunities to further embed the LPs in everyday leadership and management practice
