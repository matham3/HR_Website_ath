What is Performance Management at KAUST?

Performance management at KAUST is a continuous partnership between you, your manager, and the University. It connects your individual contribution to KAUST's strategic priorities while supporting your personal growth and career development.

Through the PMP, we align personal objectives with divisional and University-wide strategic goals, ensuring everyone's efforts contribute meaningfully to KAUST's mission. This alignment creates clarity, purpose, and impact in your role.

Performance is a combination of Results and Behavior

Results are important, and they also need to be sustainable – we all need to work in a way that promotes cooperation and takes people with us rather than against us. The balance of Results and Behaviors to decide how someone is performing will depend on the role and the circumstances – this is for the manager to determine and communicate.

Results

What the person achieves against their contribution objectives

Behavior

How the person goes about achieving these results
