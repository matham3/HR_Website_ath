Our Rewards Philosophy

KAUST offers a wide range of benefits designed to provide you and your family with the lifestyle and financial security. Our competitive employee benefits include medical and dental coverage, paid annual leave and much more.

Our comprehensive benefits package is designed to support you and your family throughout your journey at KAUST, ensuring you have the resources and security you need to thrive both professionally and personally.

Note: Your eligibility for each benefit depends on your contract.
